Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 45 min Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a round cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add milk, oil, and vanilla, and mix until smooth.
  4. Stir in vinegar (the batter will foam slightly).
  5. Pour into pan and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  6. Let the cake cool before slicing and serving.

Tea

Prep Time: 10 min Serves: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons tea powder (or 2 tea bags)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons sugar (adjust as per taste)
  • 2–3 cardamom pods (optional)
  • 1 small piece of ginger, crushed (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil Water: Bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add Flavors (Optional): Add crushed ginger and cardamom.
  3. Add Tea Powder: Add tea and simmer 2–3 minutes.
  4. Add Milk & Sugar: Boil another 2–3 minutes.
  5. Simmer: Adjust to preferred strength and color.
  6. Strain & Serve: Strain into cups and enjoy.
